K Road Rabbits...

is a rabbit breeding program that breeds rabbits of the English Spot, Himalayan, and Polish breeds. We also share rabbit facts, videos, and pictures.

We are a rural Kansas based program that breeds and sells rabbits. We sell rabbits as pets and enjoy showing animals to local people and at rabbit shows.

Our group consists of Eve, our rabbit breeder, caretaker and ceo; Daphne, assistant rabbit breeder and caretaker; Jeremiah, our editor; and Azariah, our coder. You can find us on social media, on Facebook and Instagram @K Rd Rabbits.
